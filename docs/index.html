PLEASE NOTE: The application is still [Jan 2019] under development. If you would like to use it, please do so at YOUR OWN RISK and the assessment should be treated as INDICATIVE. You need to carry out a chemical test of your well water to be sure. It is estimated that ~60% of 10 million hand pumped tubewells in rural Bangladesh remained untested for arsenic. This accounts for widespread exposure to this insidious toxin and resulting in multi-systemic effects including cancer. Unfortunately, there is no specific treatment but drinking arsenic-safe water. Using this web application a tubewell can easily be assessed for arsenic. All you need to provide your Upazila name, colour of the tubewell-platform, and select a depth of tubewell. Please also tell us whether the tubewell water is used for drinking.
